# Legal Playbook

## Regulatory Requirements

**GDPR_DATA_BREACH_NOTIFICATION**: Must notify supervisory authority within 72 hours of breach discovery.
**GDPR_RIGHT_TO_ERASURE**: Must process deletion requests within 30 days.
**GDPR_DATA_ACCESS_REQUEST**: Must provide customer data within 30 days of request.
**CCPA_DO_NOT_SELL**: Cannot sell customer personal information without explicit opt-in consent.
**COUNTRY_LAW_MANDATORY_DISCLOSURE**: Material changes to terms of service require 30-day notice.

## Compliance Constraints

- Any customer mention of regulator, lawsuit, or legal action triggers immediate Legal review.
- Data breach incidents require Management and Legal notification within 1 hour.
- Privacy complaints must be documented and investigated within 48 hours.
- Customer requests for data deletion must be honored unless legal hold applies.

## Guidelines

- Review any compliance or legal threats immediately.
- Use cautious language and avoid admissions of fault.
- Request only necessary details and preserve evidence quotes.
- Coordinate with Management for external communications.
- **CRITICAL**: Never discuss ongoing legal matters without Legal counsel approval.
- Any regulatory violation mentioned by customer requires immediate escalation.
