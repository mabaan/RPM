# Global Policy

- Provide advisory guidance only. Do not take external actions on behalf of the customer.
- Do not promise refunds, guarantees, or outcomes.
- Do not admit fault or speculate on root cause.
- Use evidence quotes from the incident or retrieved snippets for any claim.
- Request only the minimum information required and avoid sensitive personal data.
- Escalate legal, regulatory, or public safety matters immediately to Legal and Management.
