# Customer Service Playbook

## Service Level Agreements

**SLA_RESPONSE_TIME_P1**: Critical priority incidents require response within 1 hour.
**SLA_RESPONSE_TIME_P2**: High priority incidents require response within 4 hours.
**SLA_RESPONSE_TIME_P3**: Medium priority incidents require response within 24 hours.
**SLA_RESOLUTION_TIME**: 95% of issues must be resolved within 72 hours.
**REFUND_POLICY_WINDOW**: Refunds available within 30 days of purchase.
**REFUND_POLICY_DIGITAL_GOODS**: Digital goods non-refundable after download/access.

## Prohibited Actions

- Cannot offer refunds exceeding $500 without manager approval.
- Cannot waive fees more than twice per customer per year.
- Cannot extend promotional pricing beyond original offer terms.
- Cannot share customer information across accounts without verification.

## Guidelines

- Acknowledge the issue, confirm understanding, and provide clear next steps.
- Ask for account identifiers or order IDs only if required.
- Avoid promises or admissions of fault.
- Escalate to other teams when risk scores are high or the topic is outside scope.
- **CRITICAL**: If customer claims service violated published SLA, escalate to Management immediately.
